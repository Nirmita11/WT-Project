Things to do in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world and a global icon. Truly a feat of engineering, the building represents the conceptual heart and soul of the city of Dubai.

Dubai Mall

This downtown mall is known for luxury stores like Cartier and Harry Winston. It also has an aquarium, ice rink, and 360-degree views of the city from the world's tallest building, The Burj Khalifa.

Palm Jumeirah

The iconic manmade island of Palm Jumeirah is famous for its trademark palm tree shape and houses waterfront bungalows, plush boutiques, luxury hotels and upscale restaurants.

Burj Al Arab

Jumeirah Burj Al Arab is urenowned for its 'seven-star status'. The luxury record-breaking all-suite hotel includes a range of fine-dining restaurants, incredible beaches and the expansive Burj Al Arab Terrace.
