Things to do in Singapore

Singapore Changi Airport

Singapore Changi Airport is the primary international airport that serves the country of Singapore, and is one of the largest transportation hubs in Asia.

Singapore Cable Car

The Singapore Cable Car is a gondola lift in Singapore, providing an aerial link from Mount Faber on Singapore Island to the resort island of Sentosa across the Keppel Harbour.

Singapore Botanic Gardens

The Singapore Botanic Gardens is a 166-year-old tropical garden located at the fringe of the Orchard Road shopping district in Singapore.
