Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a famous wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France. It was built between 1887 and 1889 by Gustave Eiffel's company.

Sainte-Chapelle

Built by King Louis IX to house a relic of the crown of thorns the 13th-century Sainte Chapelle is renowned for its striking gothic architecture and some of the most exquisite stained glass windows in Europe.

Arc De Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe honors those who fought and died for France during the French Revolution and the Napoleonic Wars, with the names of all French victories and generals inscribed on its inner and outer surfaces.
